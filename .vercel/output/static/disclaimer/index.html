Our quiz reports, planning guides, and calculator outputs are educational tools designed to help you identify common planning gaps — they are not professional assessments, certified analyses, or individualized recommendations.

Longevity and Life Expectancy Calculator Disclosures

Any life expectancy, longevity, or survival probability tools on our platform use publicly available actuarial data from sources including the Social Security Administration (SSA), the CDC, and USALEEP county-level mortality data. These tools produce statistical probability estimates based on population cohorts with similar demographic characteristics. They are not medical diagnoses, actuarial certifications, or life insurance underwriting assessments.

Outputs are presented as survival probability curves or probability ranges — not as a prediction of any individual's lifespan. We do not use the phrase "your life expectancy is" or similar language implying a definitive personal prediction. Before relying on any longevity estimate for planning purposes, consult a licensed financial planner or actuary.

Florida Residents: Our longevity and life expectancy tools are provided for general educational purposes only. If you are a Florida resident, please be aware that we have sought legal counsel regarding Florida's life expectancy provider registration requirements (Fla. Admin. Code R. 69O-204.201) and have structured our outputs accordingly.
